
**Supported formats:** .jpg, .jpeg, .png

**Recommended size:** 800x600px or higher (aspect ratio 4:3 or 16:9 works best)

### How to Add the Image:
1. Save your award photo as `director-of-the-year.jpg`
2. Place it in the `public/images/jci/` folder
3. The image will automatically display on the community details page

**Note:** If the image is not found, it will show a fallback placeholder image.
